The Complete Guide to Choosing Hijama Cups

Choosing the right hijama cup is one of the most important decisions a practitioner makes. The wrong cup can compromise suction, visibility, and patient comfort. This guide breaks down every type available so you can make a confident, informed choice.

Standard Transparent Cups

Standard PVC cups are the workhorse of any hijama clinic. Made from medical-grade PVC, they offer good visibility, reliable suction, and are available in sizes 1–6. Indian-made standard cups are particularly popular for their durability and cost-effectiveness at high volumes.

Premium Transparent Cups

Premium cups feature ultra-clear medical PVC, superior valve grip, and tighter tolerances. If you need maximum visibility during dry cupping or want to show the cupping process to students, premium cups are the right choice. Detoxy Premium cups are manufactured to clinic-grade standards.

Silicone Moving Cups

Silicone cups don't require a pump — simply squeeze and place. They're ideal for gliding massage, lymphatic drainage, and facial therapy. The soft material conforms naturally to curved body areas like the shoulders and calves.

Fire Cupping Glass Sets

Traditional borosilicate glass fire cups create suction through heat rather than a vacuum pump. Preferred by practitioners of classical hijama, fire cupping produces a distinctive deep draw. Our 16-piece glass set covers all major body zones with 4 size variants.

Which Cup Is Right for You?

For a new clinic: start with a standard cup assortment (sizes 1–6) plus a magnetic vacuum kit. For facial therapy: add a silicone facial set of 4 or 7. For traditional practice: consider the fire cupping glass set. Always keep sterile consumables — blades, gloves, and cotton — stocked at all times.
