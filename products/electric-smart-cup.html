Frequently Asked Questions

How many suction levels does the Electric Smart Cupping Device have?

The Detoxy Hijama Electric Smart Cupping Device features 9 adjustable suction levels, allowing practitioners and users to precisely calibrate pressure from gentle relaxation therapy to deep clinical cupping. The LCD display shows the current suction level and battery status in real-time.

Does the electric cupping device have heat therapy?

Yes. The device includes built-in heat therapy with temperatures ranging from 40°C to 50°C. Heat combined with suction dramatically enhances therapeutic benefits — improving circulation, relaxing muscle spasms, and providing relief for chronic pain conditions more effectively than suction alone.

How long does the battery last on the Electric Smart Cupping Device?

The 2000mAh rechargeable battery provides approximately 60–90 minutes of continuous use on a full charge, depending on suction level and heat setting. The device charges via USB-C and typically reaches full charge in 2–3 hours. An LCD battery indicator shows remaining charge.

Is the electric cupping device suitable for home use?

Yes, the Detoxy Electric Smart Cupping Device is specifically designed for both clinic and home use. Its intuitive one-touch controls, automatic pressure regulation, and LCD display make it accessible to users without professional training. Simply select a suction level, apply the cup, and let the device do the work.

Can the electric cupping device be used for facial cupping?

The electric smart cupping device is primarily designed for body cupping therapy (back, shoulders, legs, arms). For facial cupping, we recommend our dedicated silicone facial cup sets, which offer the gentle, controlled suction appropriate for delicate facial tissue.
