What is Hijama Cupping Therapy?

Hijama (Al-Hijama) is an ancient Islamic prophetic medicine practice involving placement of cups on the skin to create suction. Recommended by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, this healing tradition has been practiced for over 1,400 years. Today, hijama is widely practiced across India by Muslim practitioners, wellness centers, physiotherapists and holistic health clinics.

Dry cupping uses suction without incisions — safe for home use. Wet cupping (hijama bi-shurt) involves superficial incisions for blood-letting, performed only by trained practitioners. Both forms require high-quality cups like the Detoxy Hijama 24-Set Magnetic Vacuum Cupping Kit for safe, effective therapy.
