How We Started

From a Small Workshop to India's Leading Manufacturer

Detoxy Hijama was founded in 2020 by Mohamed Aasiq in Pollachi, Coimbatore — the heartland of Tamil Nadu. What started as a small manufacturing unit with a vision to provide affordable, high-quality hijama cups to local practitioners has grown into a trusted name across India.

We saw that most hijama practitioners were paying inflated prices through distributors and resellers. Our answer was simple — manufacture directly and sell directly. No middlemen. No markups. Just factory-fresh quality delivered to your door.

Today, over 5,000 practitioners, clinics, therapists and hospitals across India trust Detoxy Hijama for their cupping therapy supplies.
