How to Source Bulk Hijama Supplies at Wholesale Prices

Running a hijama clinic means managing consumable costs tightly. Buying direct from the manufacturer — rather than through distributors — can cut your per-unit costs by 30–50%. Here's how to do it.

Why Buy Direct from Manufacturers

Distributors and medical supply stores add margins of 20–40% on top of manufacturer prices. By contacting the manufacturer directly — like Detoxy Hijama — you get factory pricing, consistent quality, and the ability to request custom quantities.

What to Buy in Bulk

High-turnover consumables are the best candidates for bulk purchase: surgical blades (No.11, packs of 100), latex gloves (packs of 100), surgical cotton (400g rolls), and standard or premium cups. These items have long shelf lives and are used in every session.

Minimum Order Quantities

Most manufacturers set MOQs. For Detoxy Hijama, cups start from 1 piece at the standard price with bulk discounts at 300–500 units. Consumables like blades and gloves have bulk pricing at 10 packs and above. Always ask about tiered pricing.

How to Place a Bulk Order

Use our Bulk Quote form to describe your requirements — product types, quantities, and delivery timeline. We typically respond within 4 business hours with a detailed price sheet. Payment can be made via bank transfer, UPI, or cash on delivery for established clinics.

Storing Your Supplies

Store blades and gloves in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Cotton rolls should be kept sealed until use. Hijama cups should be sterilised between uses with 70% isopropyl alcohol or autoclave for glass cups.
